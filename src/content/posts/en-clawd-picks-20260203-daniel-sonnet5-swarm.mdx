## Why This Matters

If this feature ships, Claude Code evolves from "a helpful assistant" to "a team of assistants." You get:

1. **Parallel task execution** — No more waiting for one task to finish before starting the next
2. **Isolated contexts** — Each sub-agent focuses on its domain without stepping on others' toes
3. **Background processing** — Keep working while agents handle builds, tests, or other time-consuming tasks in the background

We're basically turning "workflow" into "work-swarm."
