## The Study

- **Subjects**: 200 employees at a U.S. technology company
- **Duration**: April to December 2025 (9 months)
- **Method**: Observation + interviews + data tracking
- **Institution**: UC Berkeley Haas School of Business

This isn't a self-report survey where people say "yeah AI helps me work better." The researchers actually observed how these employees' work patterns changed over time.
