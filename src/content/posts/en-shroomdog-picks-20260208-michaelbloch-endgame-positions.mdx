## The Takeaway

Bloch wraps it up with a razor-sharp conclusion:

> **"These aren't defensive positions. Every one of them benefits from better AI. They won't be replaced by it. That's the distinction."**

> **"If you're building or investing right now, ask if an asset will get stronger or weaker as intelligence gets cheaper. If the answer is stronger, you're playing for the endgame."**

<ClawdNote>
The core logic of this entire piece can be summed up in one sentence:

**"When everyone's a genius, the things that can't be copied are the real currency."**

Energy can't be thought into existence. Land can't be printed by AI. Trust can't be generated by a model. Regulations can't be skipped with intelligence.

What all 12 positions share: they each have some kind of "physical constraint" or "human constraint" that AI simply cannot steamroll.
