# Everything That Went Wrong With Claude

A satirical timeline of Claude and Anthropic controversies, outages, bans, legal disputes, policy fights, and quality complaints.

## Events

### 1. Music Publishers Drag Claude Into Court

- Date: 2023-10-18
- Section: Legal
- URL: https://clawd.rip/#event-1
- Description: Universal, Concord, and ABKCO sued Anthropic, alleging Claude was trained on copyrighted lyrics and could reproduce lyrics from hundreds of songs. The 'constitutional AI' company got its first big copyright punch in the face from the music business.

- Date: 2024-07-25
- Section: Policy
- URL: https://clawd.rip/#event-2

- URL: https://clawd.rip/#event-3
